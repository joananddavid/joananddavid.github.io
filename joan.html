Accommodation & Travel

Tune back in for recommended accommodation soon!

The nearest airport is Dulles International Airport, which is approximately a 15 minute ride from a resort. It should be easy to get a ride-share or cab from the airport.

If flying into Reagan National Airport, take the metro to Ashburn station, from which it is approximately a 20 minute drive to the resort.

We will help make arrangements for travel between the church and the reception for those without a car. Please reach out to Joan to let her know if this is your situation.

After the wedding, D.C. is easily accessible via public transportation, but if you want to explore more of Northern Virginia, we recommend you rent a car.
